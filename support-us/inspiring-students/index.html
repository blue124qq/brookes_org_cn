      • Inspiring students to become the best they can be

        Because... connecting you with our talented students helps them to excel. Supporting our students to understand the communities in which they will work enables them to make a real impact when they graduate.

        You can pass on your invaluable insights and provide experience and opportunities to our students.

        How you can help us to inspire students

        By working together, we can offer our students experiences beyond their studies. You can support us by:

        • offering volunteering opportunities or supporting our award winning programme to train and develop social entrepreneurs
        • becoming a mentor, offering work placements or internships or recruiting our students when they graduate
        • participating in advisory boards, helping inform curriculum developments or running alumni networks and events.

        Please contact us to discuss other opportunities.


      • Your support, our success

        • Offering real life experiences

          Our students have the vision and energy to make a real impact on local communities and the wider world through volunteering opportunities or as budding social entrepreneurs.

        • Boosting graduate employability

          Our students are tomorrow’s workforce and have outstanding potential. Mentoring and work placement opportunities are vital components in this process.

        • Supporting the Brookes experience

          Your views, insights and knowledge are invaluable to us as we continue to evolve the Brookes experience. Read here about how you could get involved.
