    • Partnerships

      Oxford Brookes University has established partnerships with many primary, secondary and special schools – and an increasing number of other organisations – throughout the region who work with university tutors to deliver the school experience and practical dimension of the Initial Teacher Education programmes.

      To complement our partnership activities we also run a range of continuing professional development courses and host regular conferences and seminars .
