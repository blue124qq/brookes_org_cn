About Clearing

Clearing 2021 will open on 6 July and run until the end of September. You can apply for a course using Clearing if you’re not already holding an offer from a university or college and the course still has places.

Clearing provides you with an opportunity if you have:  

  • decided to apply directly to university
  • changed your mind about what or where you want to study
  • missed your grades
  • not got the offer you want
  • decided to give university a go.  

In July 2021 we will release a list of our courses that have spaces available. Vacancy lists will also be available from 6 July on the UCAS website. 

If you would like to apply to Oxford Brookes before Clearing, you can search and apply for courses for September 2021 entry as part of the main application cycle.

Contact us

If you'd like to find out more about applying to Oxford Brookes, there are a number of ways you can get in touch with us: 

  • Need advice or help with your application? Talk to our friendly admissions staff at admissions@brookes.org.cn or call 0345 350 3699 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm).
  • Got a question about the course you are interested in? We are here to help. Simply fill out this form and we will pass your query on to the right person.  
  • Want to find out more about student life at Oxford Brookes? Chat to our students in our online chat service, run by students, for students.
