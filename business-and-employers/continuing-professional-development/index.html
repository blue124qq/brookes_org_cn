      Continuing professional development

      • Continuing professional development

        Investing in your workforce to develop skills, improve leadership, and increase employee loyalty is one of the best things a business can do to enhance its performance.

        Oxford Brookes can support you in developing your junior, middle, and senior managers with a broad offering of courses and training programmes across general business skills and industry-specific qualifications.

        Our courses include:

        • Short courses designed as an intensive introduction to the subject being studied, often leading to a recognised qualification
        • Accredited courses, recognised by industry bodies
        • Bespoke courses, designed and developed for your specific business requirements by a team of Oxford Brookes specialists

        Some of our courses are delivered through our specialist centres. You can explore existing courses below, or contact us if you would like to discuss a customised course for your business.
