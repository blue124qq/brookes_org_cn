        • Courses

          The course you choose will be at the heart of your university experience.

          Its what youll probably spend most of your time at Oxford Brookes thinking about! Its certainly a choice you need to carefully consider. Our courses are designed to help you make the most of the opportunities open to you and achieve your best.

          Use these pages to find out which course is best suited to you.

      • Find your course

      • Undergraduate

        Explore the possibilities with degree courses designed to support you in shaping your pathway to success. An undergraduate degree from Oxford Brookes helps you take the first step in creating your future.

        Postgraduate

        Whether youre pursuing a passion, furthering your career or moving in a new direction, Oxford Brookes can offer you excellence in postgraduate study. Browse research degrees and taught courses on our postgraduate pages.

      • Courses at our partner colleges

        Find out about opportunities for learning close to home with variety, flexibility and great support. You can also consider your options for topping up a foundation degree to a full honours degree.

        Professional and short courses

        Discover our flexible approach to a wide range of Continuing Professional Development courses, from short courses to part-time masters level qualifications.

      • Foundation courses

        Take the next step as a school-leaver to meet the entry requirements needed for degree level study in a particular subject area.

        International pathway courses

        Prepare for undergraduate and postgraduate study in the UK. Improve English skills and prepare for a degree in your chosen subject, and get an introduction to university life.
