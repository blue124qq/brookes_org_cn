      Brookes Lettings

      • Brookes Lettings

        Do you have property to rent in Oxford or Swindon? If so then get in touch with us. We are currently looking for landlords to join our housing list for the academic year starting in September. For more information, visit the links below. 

        Brookes Lettings aims to lease quality properties to let to our students. When you lease your property to us, we become your tenant, taking on all the associated responsibilities. This means you don’t have to worry about finding tenants, your rent is guaranteed, tenancy issues are managed, damages are sorted out and we help keep you compliant with legislation.

        You can either keep control of the maintenance of the property, or we have our own staff and contractors who can do it for you.

        Our interest is in providing our students with safe housing for them to undertake their studies, not turning a profit. We won’t charge you any extra fees or administration costs.
