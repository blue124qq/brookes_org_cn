Open hours

Visitor parking is bookable between 7:30 to 16:30, Monday to Friday.

Bookings staying after this time will continue to be honoured for the rest of the day.

Outside of these times, some University car parks are available for public use. Parking machines and tariffs boards are located in the car parks as tariff fees may be applicable for the duration of stay.

 

Contact us

Visitor car parking spaces are limited and subject to availability. Staff/ Departments will need to book their guest an allocated visitor car parking space.

These spaces are offered to book on a first-come-first-served basis and are available 12 weeks in advance.

For travel to campus related queries, please contact Transport Services on transport@brookes.org.cn

Announcements

Visitor car parking is unavailable during the graduation period (21st -25th June 2020)

The University encourages the use of sustainable travel methods, especially when visitors are travelling to campuses inside the Oxford ring road. Local Park & rides and public transport options (such as the BROOKESbus) can facilitate travel to the University campuses.

 

Oxford Brookes University staff can check visitor parking availability by logging into the system and selecting the applicable campus and date.

If a space is available, the booking process is easy and booking confirmation will be sent directly to your visitor email address. This booking must be displayed on the car dash for the entire duration of the visit.

If visitor car parking is at capacity for the times required, visitor car parking would be unavailable and the individual would be unable to park on campus. Alternative public transport or local public car parking options may be available, to facilitate travel to the campus.

Vehicles displaying a valid council issued Blue Badge, will be permitted in designated accessible parking bays. These spaces are offered on a first come first served basis and are not bookable.

If a booking is no longer required or details need amending, the staff member who originally booked the space will need to access the visitor car parking system, find their booking and make amendments/ cancellations.

Help

If you encounter any technical issues with the system please contact the IT Service Desk on x3311 or via their portal at https://service.brookes.org.cn/brookes

Please quote 'Visitor Parking' when raising your incident.

Policies

This service is provided by Estates and Campus Services at Oxford Brookes University.

Only Oxford Brookes University staff members are permitted to book spaces for their visitors using this system. Staff are NOT permitted to book spaces for their own use.
