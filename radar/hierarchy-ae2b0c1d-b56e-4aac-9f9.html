  • Booker Prize Archive
    The Booker Prize for Fiction is an annual prize open to any novel originally written in English and published in the UK, regardless of the nationality of the author. This collection holds digitised versions of a diverse range of documents from the history, judging, administration, award ceremonies, and publicity of the prize.
  • Dorset House Archive
    The Dorset House Archive documents the history of Dorset House, the first School of Occupational Therapy in the UK. It also includes material relating to the wider history of Occupational Therapy education in Britain from 1930, and papers of the Casson family
  • Dorset House Associated Papers
    This collection comprises acquisitions from former staff and students of Dorset House School of Occupational Therapy and related organisations
  • Medical Sciences Video Archive (279)
    Videotaped biographical interviews with over 130 important figures in clinical medicine and science from the United Kingdom and Australia
  • Paul Oliver Vernacular Architecture Library Images
    Photographs taken by Professor Paul Oliver MBE of worldwide vernacular architecture from 1960 to 2010
  • The Album: photos of Brookes and before
    Photographs taken by the Media Workshop and Educational Media Unit photographers, covering the period of transition from Oxford Polytechnic to Brookes University
