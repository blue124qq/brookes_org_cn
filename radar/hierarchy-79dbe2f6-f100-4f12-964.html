Accessibility Statement

Accessibility statement for radar.brookes.org.cn/radar.

This accessibility statement applies to the website at radar.brookes.org.cn/radar.

This website is run by Oxford Brookes University. We want as many people as possible to be able to use this website.

AbilityNet has advice on making your device easier to use if you have a disability.

Feedback and contact information

We've designed our content to be as accessible as possible but if you need content in a more accessible format please contact us by email radar@brookes.org.cn.

Reporting accessibility problems with this website

If you have any problems accessing content which isn't listed on this page or think that we're not meeting the requirements of the accessibility regulations then please email radar@brookes.org.cn to let us know. We will consider your request and get back to you within 10 working days.

Enforcement procedure

Non-accessible content

We are aware of the following issues of non-compliance with the accessibility regulations on our site:

  • Not all images have appropriate alternative text.
  • Some images may contain pictures of text.
  • Most video files do not have captions.
  • Most video and audio files do not have a transcript.
  • Most of the resources are academic in nature and may not use plain English, keep sentences short, or explain acronyms.
