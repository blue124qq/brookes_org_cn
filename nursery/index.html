  • Oxford Brookes University Nursery

    We offer high-quality childcare for children aged 4 months to 5 years. Priority is given to children of University staff and students but external children are also welcomed.

    We are a well-established setting with a team of highly-qualified and experienced staff, several of whom have been with us for many years.

    We were judged ‘Good’ by Ofsted under their new rigorous inspection framework in October 2019. "Children benefit from a broad and interesting curriculum... Children show high levels of wellbeing and are confident, happy and relaxed at nursery. By the time children leave nursery to start school, they are well prepared for the move and have developed a positive disposition towards learning." (Ofsted, October 2019)
